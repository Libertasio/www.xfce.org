Xfce 4.2 trailer
 

Simon Meurer has created a short trailer
to introduce Xfce 4.2.0 to the world! The
music was composed by Marten Hiekmann.

High Bandwidth Version (11.2 MB)
Low Bandwidth Version (3.6 MB)

The files are in WMV format (both Xine and Mplayer can
play that using the free ffmpeg codecs). The reason for
choosing WMV was that our attempts to use plain MPEG
files resulted in too large videos, and QuickTime is not
supported very well by free media players.

Please send feedback and comments concerning this
trailer to Simon Meurer (simon4ever at gmx dot de).
